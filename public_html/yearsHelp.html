These are the valid formats for year values:

  • A single year: 1979
  • An exact range: 2001-2003
  • An range ending in a particular year: -2002 (i.e., all years up to 2002).
  • A range starting in a particular year: 1975- (i.e., all years from 1975 to the present).
  • A range of years up to the present: +3 (i.e., 2004-2007).
All years are assumed to be C.E.
Years can contain only 1-4 digits, plus sign ("+"), minus sign/hyphen ("-").
If you enter multiple years/ranges, separate the distinct values/ranges with semi-colons (";"): 1970;1980;1990.
