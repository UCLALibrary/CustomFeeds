There are three options of viewing your feed:

  • View directly in the browser: modern browsers (e.g., Internet Explorer 7+) will style the feed and provide subscription options. Older browsers will display the raw XML coding for the feed.
  • View (or subscribe) via My Yahoo! The feed will be passed to Yahoo's reader, where it will be styled and you will be able to subscribe. You will need a free My Yahoo! account to subscribe.
  • View (or subscribe) via Google. The feed will be passed to Google's reader, where it will be styled and you will be able to subscribe. You will need a free Google account to subscribe.
